While PdfDroplet can work with pages of any size, the following illustrates using pages laid out as A5 pages for printing on A4 paper (A4 is twice the size of A5).

Step 1: 

Prepare your book using a word processor
or page layout application, using A5 pages.

Step 2:

Create a PDF of the document using one of the
many tools which do that, either from Adobe,
Microsoft, BullZip, or other source.

Step 3:

Drop that PDF onto PdfDroplet, which create a new PDF document sized for A4 paper. The pages are reordered so that they will make a booklet when you fold down the middle.

Step 4:

Print the booklet PDF on both sides of the paper. Fold down the middle, then fasten the edge with staples or thread.
